# Flower Prediction Experiment

## Status: human-tested

## Description
Replication of Study 1a from a paper on flower petal spot prediction. Participants see a distribution of flowers with varying petal spots (0-5), make a prediction about a new flower, and estimate the mean number of spots. Includes comprehension checks and demographics.

## Plan
- Single-page scenario with bar chart showing flower distribution
- Prediction task: "How many spots will the next flower have?" (0-5, decimal allowed)
- Mean estimation: "What is the average number of spots?" (0-5, decimal allowed)
- Comprehension checks: total flowers, flowers with 5 spots
- Demographics: age, sex, education (all optional)
- Debrief: study purpose explanation
